About Me

Hello! I am a game programmer with expertise in the Unity engine, gameplay programming, graphics, shaders, physics, optimisation, and more. I enjoy collaborating with across disciplines to deliver the best possible experience for players. I have been working as a programmer at Coatsink Software since March 2021.

I am also a composer - I love using acoustic instruments and electronics to create emotive, challenging music. My work has been featured by the Britten Sinfonia, SEAGER gallery, Trinity Laban Conservatoire, and more.

You can find all my personal software projects at github.com/chris-kirkham, and email me at chris.kirkham11@gmail.com
